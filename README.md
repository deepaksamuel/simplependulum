# simplependulum
videos of pendulum oscillations

## Parameters (approximate only)

- Length of rope ~3.5 m
- Time period ~ 4 s
- Bin width ~50 cm
- Extent of oscillations: 300 cm (from one side to other)

## Dataset from video by Merric
The used frames are from 140-1191

- Total frames: 1453  

- Useful: 935 (about 9 oscillations, check in Google sheets as well.)
